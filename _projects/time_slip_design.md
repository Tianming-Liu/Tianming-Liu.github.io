<div class="project-header">
  <h1>Time Slip</h1>
  
  <div class="project-meta">
    <p><strong>Dynamic Simulation & Parametric Design & Design Visualization</strong></p>
    <p><span class="highlight-name">LIU TIANMING</span>; HU ZHEN; HUO ZIXUAN; XIE YUJING; LIU PEIYUAN; WANG QING; MA CHAO; CAO FEIWEI</p>
    <p>2021/02 - 2021/09</p>
    <p>Chengdu, China</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="project-description">
    <p>
      This project demonstrates a comprehensive parametric design workflow using Rhino and 
      Grasshopper, covering design, visualization, and construction phases.
    </p>
    <p>
      The design phase employed <code>Kangaroo</code> Grasshopper physics simulation engine
      for structural generation and optimization.<code>Grasshopper</code> was also used to create 
      animations for model presentations and construction processing.
      During the construction phase, the parametric model generated detailed fabrication drawings
      for irregular bamboo poles and exported positioning data for complex joint connections,
      providing critical support for construction accuracy and assembly coordination.
    </p>
    <p>
      I was responsible for all Grasshopper algorithm development, 
      implementing the complete parametric logic for dynamic simulation, visualization workflows, 
      and fabrication drawing throughout the project.
    </p>
  </div>
