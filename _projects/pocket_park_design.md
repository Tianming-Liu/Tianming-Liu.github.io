<div class="project-header">
  <h1>Pocket Park Generation System</h1>
  
  <div class="project-meta">
    <p><strong>Urban Data Analysis & Data-driven Design</strong></p>
    <p>Individual Work</p>
    <p>2022/06 - 2022/11</p>
    <p>Shenzhen, China</p>
  </div>
  
  <div class="project-description">
    <p>
      Nowadays, with the vigorous development of <code>algorithms and urban data</code>, 
      designers have gained more means and richer perspectives to understand the site. 
      However, large-scale analysis is often difficult to guide specific spatial generation, 
      and a large number of data analysis results are difficult to be directly translated into actionable design strategies.
    </p>
    <p>
      This project attempts to provide a solution to the above problems through a system 
      covering the work from <code>quick site interpretation</code> based on urban data 
      to the whole <code>design generation</code> for pocket park.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
